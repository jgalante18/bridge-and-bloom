⚠ Academic Disclaimer

This is a Fictional Practice

Last updated: 2025  •  Created for educational purposes only

Nature of This Website

Bridge & Bloom is a fictional private practice created solely for academic purposes as part of a graduate-level psychology program. This website, including all content, services described, contact information, and branding, is a simulated exercise and does not represent a real, operating psychological practice.

No Real Services Are Offered

Nothing on this website constitutes an offer to provide psychological services, therapy, counseling, mental health treatment, or any other professional services. Specifically:

  • No actual therapeutic relationship is established by visiting or interacting with this site.
  • No licensed clinician is available through this website.
  • Any contact form submissions are for demonstration purposes only and will not be reviewed by a mental health professional.
  • Pricing, availability, and service descriptions are entirely fictional.

Not a Substitute for Professional Care

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis or are in need of psychological support, please reach out to a qualified mental health professional or an emergency service in your area. The following resources are available 24/7:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency Services: Call 911 if you or someone else is in immediate danger

Intellectual Property & Academic Use

All content, design, and branding associated with Bridge & Bloom - including the name, color palette, logo, and copy - was developed as a student project. This work is intended exclusively for academic evaluation and educational demonstration. It is not intended for commercial use.

Accuracy of Information

While every effort has been made to include accurate and representative information about therapeutic modalities (such as CBT and ACT), this content is provided for illustrative purposes only and should not be used as a clinical reference. Descriptions of services and approaches are simplified for a general audience and are not clinically exhaustive.

Summary: Bridge & Bloom is a mock website created by a Doctorate of Psychology student for a private practice class. No real services are offered. No therapeutic relationship is formed. If you are in crisis, please contact 988 or your local emergency services.
